## Risk Disclosure

40 Acres is experimental software. Use it at your own risk.

Key risks include: smart contract bugs despite audits, underlying DEX failure, reward variability reducing repayment pace, USDC depeg, and concentrated DEX exposure. The 80% utilization cap and multi-DEX expansion are the primary risk management mechanisms.
