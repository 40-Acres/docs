# 🫏 40 Acres Finance

40 Acres is a cashflow lending protocol that underwrites loans on revenue generating assets. Deposit a yield-bearing asset, starting with veNFTs, and borrow against the revenue it generates. Your collateral earns rewards every epoch. Those rewards repay your loan automatically.

No interest. No liquidations. No monthly payments.

## Key Features

**Self-repaying**: Your weekly epoch rewards flow directly toward your loan balance. You don't have to do anything. The loan repays itself over time.

**Interest-free**: There is no interest rate on your debt. The only cost is a one-time origination fee and the spread between your rewards and what the protocol retains; in addition to 25% of your weekly rewards generated every week.
