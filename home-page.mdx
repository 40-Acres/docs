# 🫏 40 Acres Finance

40 Acres is a cashflow lending protocol that underwrites loans on revenue generating assets. Deposit an asset, and borrow against the revenue it generates. Your collateral earns rewards every epoch. Those rewards repay your loan automatically.

No additional interest. No monthly payments. Automated repayments.
